# The Living Chronicles

> **Every World Writes Its Own Legend.**

> *We believe the best online worlds aren't built by developers alone. They are shaped by the communities that inhabit them.*

A modern, community-driven browser RPG inspired by the spirit of Legend of the Green Dragon.

The Living Chronicles is not a remake.

It is a new foundation for persistent online worlds where every server can become its own unique realm, shaped by its community, its stories, and eventually its own plugins.

---

## 🌍 Vision

Most modern online games are built around one central world.

The Living Chronicles takes a different approach.

Instead of one official universe, it enables **many independent worlds**, each with its own identity, lore, rules, economy, and community.

Every server should feel like a place with its own history.

Not because the developers wrote every story.

But because the players did.

---

## ❤️ What Makes This Different?

This project is inspired by the culture that made **Legend of the Green Dragon** special.

Thousands of players didn't remember LoGD because of its combat system.

They remembered:

- the people
- the guild rivalries
- the inside jokes
- the custom events
- the worlds created by passionate server administrators

The Living Chronicles is built to preserve exactly that spirit while providing a modern, maintainable foundation.

---

## ✨ Core Principles

- 🌱 **Community over optimization**
- 🏰 **Many worlds instead of one**
- 🔌 **Plugin-ready by design**
- 📖 **Stories created by players**
- ⚔️ **Meaningful choices over endless grinding**
- ❤️ **Long-term persistence over seasonal resets**

---

## 🌌 The Demo World

The first world is a classic fantasy setting with its own twist.

Centuries ago, an event known only as **The Fracture** changed reality itself.

An ancient civilization disappeared.

Its technology fell silent.

Magic awakened.

Now adventurers explore forgotten ruins, uncover impossible relics and fight creatures that never existed before the world changed.
